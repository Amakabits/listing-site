About Us

Jerclems Investment Limited is a Leading Real Estate Development Company you can trust.
We buy, sell and develop landed properties in Nigeria. Over the years we have completed over 706 projects and sold over 2000 acres of lands across the country.
We sell only government allocated lands.

Mr. Jerome Uzonna Okafor, Managing Director.

He is an entrepreneur, real estate professional. He has uninterrupted work experience of over 20 years in real estate development.
His expertise, knowledge and experience that cut across development concept and implementation, Real estate investment and marketing, property finance and real estate advisory. He is the managing director of Jerclems Investments Ltd and also partner with many other real estate company.
He holds Bsc. in Geology from University Of Ibadan and he has attended both international and local real estate training, workshop and seminar.
